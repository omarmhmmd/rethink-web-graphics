Rethink: Web

  • About

    As the internet proliferated over the past two decades certain design patterns have emerged that provide a predictable browsing experience. This serves the purpose of commerce well, a predictable vernacular means less time between finding the site and making a purchase. However, this phenomena has also served to winnow the breadth of experiences available. Many tools have been developed that provide helpful frameworks for quickly building commerce driven sites, but also served to cement a narrow user experience. What we are seeing manifest in the modern web is a platform built to serve commerce and marketing, not a platform built towards exploration, collaboration and connectivity.
